Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Introduction

Branches (“the App”) is developed by Phantom Studios. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

2. Information Collection

Branches does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No user accounts are required
  • No personal identifiers are collected

3. Local Data Storage

Any progress, preferences, or content generated within the App is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible by the developer and is never uploaded.

Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

4. Advertising

Branches displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These providers may collect limited, non-personal information such as device identifiers to serve ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Branches does not access, store, or control any data collected by advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services exclusively for advertising purposes. Their use of data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

Because Branches does not collect personal data, no user data is shared.

7. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored externally, there is no external database or backend system that could expose personal information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Branches is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
